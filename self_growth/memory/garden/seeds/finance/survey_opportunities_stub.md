
# Vetted Survey Opportunities Stub

This stub provides guidance on trusted, legitimate market research platforms that pay for survey participation. These platforms have been curated for reliability, data privacy respect, and realistic earning potential.

---

## Overview

Some users can realistically earn between **$250 and $500 per week** by steadily progressing through vetted survey platforms. These opportunities often start slow but open up access to higher-value surveys with consistent activity and accurate responses.

Raven can assist by:
- Pre-screening platform terms
- Helping fill out intake forms and profile information
- Tracking your progress across platforms
- Filtering for high-value or relevant opportunities

---

## Guided Intake

Each of the following platforms includes a suggested starting approach and key details to help you begin.

---

### 1. Prolific (https://www.prolific.com)

- **Start Type**: Academic and behavioral research surveys
- **Expected Pay**: $6–$10 per hour
- **Strength**: High trust, transparent payout, high-quality studies
- **Getting Started**:
  - Sign up and complete demographic profile thoroughly
  - Check for new studies daily or use Raven to monitor availability
  - They put you on a waitlist, so get in early and wait for their email.
---

### 2. Respondent (https://www.respondent.io)

- **Start Type**: Professional insights, industry-specific feedback
- **Expected Pay**: $20–$150 per study
- **Strength**: Fewer but higher paying studies
- **Getting Started**:
  - Use Raven to build a professional profile
  - Apply to multiple open studies weekly
  - Prepare to schedule Zoom-style interviews

---

### 3. User Interviews (https://www.userinterviews.com)

- **Start Type**: Product testing, remote interviews, surveys
- **Expected Pay**: $25–$100 per session
- **Strength**: Fast-growing, frequent studies, flexible schedule
- **Getting Started**:
  - Sign up and complete your profile
  - Mark study types you're interested in
  - Enable Raven to auto-notify when new studies match
